About Shri Panjarpol Gau-Raksha Sanstha

About us

Shri Panjarpol Gau-Raksha Sanstha is a non-governmental organization (NGO) committed to the preservation, protection, and welfare of cows. Founded on the principles of compassion, respect, and sustainability, our organization strives to create a harmonious coexistence between humans and cows. With a deep-rooted belief in the sacredness of cows, we work tirelessly to safeguard their well-being and promote their significance in society.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide comprehensive care, support, and shelter to cows, ensuring their physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being. We aim to raise awareness about the importance of cows in our culture and ecosystem, while actively working towards ending cruelty, neglect, and abandonment towards these gentle creatures.

Our Objective

  • 1. Cow Welfare

    We are dedicated to offering a safe and nurturing environment for cows, irrespective of their age, breed, or health conditions. Our organization provides nutritious food, clean water, comfortable shelter, and veterinary care to promote their overall health and happiness.

  • 2. Rescue and Rehabilitation

    We rescue cows from distress including abuse, neglect, and illegal slaughter, providing immediate medical attention and rehabilitation for reintegration into a compassionate environment.

  • 3. Education and Awareness

    We educate the community about the cultural, environmental, and economic significance of cows through workshops, seminars, and outreach programs.

  • 4. Sustainable Initiatives

    We promote organic farming, composting, and alternative energy to foster an ecosystem where cows and communities thrive together.

  • 5. Collaboration and Advocacy

    We collaborate with organizations and government bodies to advocate for stronger animal welfare laws and policies.
