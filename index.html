About us

Panjarpol Gorakshan Sanstha is a non-governmental organization (NGO) committed to the preservation, protection, and welfare of cows. Founded on the principles of compassion, respect, and sustainability, our organization strives to create a harmonious coexistence between humans and cows. With a deep-rooted belief in the sacredness of cows, we work tirelessly to safeguard their well-being and promote their significance in society.

Why Gouseva?

Cows hold a revered place in our culture, symbolizing purity, abundance, and divinity. Engaging in GoSeva allows us to express our compassion and practice ahimsa, the principle of non-violence. By providing shelter, nourishment, and medical care to cows, we ensure their well-being and protect them from harm. In doing so, we uphold our duty to show kindness to all living beings and cultivate a harmonious environment.

Join us in this noble cause of GoSeva and experience the joy that comes from serving and protecting these sacred animals. Together, let us create a world where the cows roam freely, revered and cherished for their beauty, grace, and the abundance they bring.

Benefits of Gauseva

Engaging in Gauseva, the noble act of serving and caring for cows, brings a plethora of benefits that resonate on multiple levels. It allows us to fulfill our religious and cultural duties, fostering a deep connection to our traditions. By actively participating in Gauseva initiatives, we contribute to the preservation of indigenous cow breeds, safeguarding their genetic heritage and cultural significance.

Moreover, Gauseva promotes sustainable agriculture through the utilization of cow dung for organic fertilizer and biofuel. Beyond the tangible advantages, Gauseva nurtures personal growth, gratitude, and spiritual connection, offering a transformative experience. Embracing Gauseva not only positively impacts the lives of cows but also fosters a more compassionate and harmonious society for all beings.
